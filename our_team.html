Welcome to our family!

Hello there! My name is Crystal Miles, as Team Captain and founder of our growing Walk Team, I would like to thank you for taking the time to learn about us and the cause that means so much to us. In 2008, my heart was deeply moved by the generosity and wonders of the Make-A-Wish Foundation of Michigan. Never before had I witnessed this level of sheer magic, wrapped in an abundance of generosity! These wishes can be a game changer for these beautiful children. Far more than just a nice thing to do for a child, these wishes can provide hope and courage for a child battling health issues that no one should have to bare. Parents and family members may finally have the opportunity to feel optimistic and enjoy the moment.

The Make-A-Wish Foundation of Michigan grants a little more than one wish per day, for children with a life-threatening medical condition. It is their belief that a wish experience can be a turning point for children, helping to give them the hope and encouragement needed to continue their fight! This one belief guides them in everything they do, and I can personally attest to their success!

Thus in early 2009, I was searching for a way to pay it forward and help this wonderful foundation. I found the Walk for Wishes Event on the Make-A-Wish of Michigan’s website and looked into it. Right away, I knew this was for my family. We signed up and started fundraising as best as we could. That first year we raised around $500. I was humbled by my family and friends, who were eager to help where they could and learn more about this wonderful foundation.

5 years later, the majority of my closest family and friends are now part of a still growing Walk Team and I couldn’t be prouder. I am truly blessed to have such an amazing group of people. Each team member has embraced our cause and worked tirelessly; beating the streets, working with businesses, hosting events and doing everything we can to gain donations and awareness for a cause we love.

I have been humbled by the generosity of our communities, businesses, schools and people of all ages. Every year we add more events and every year our communities embrace each one and warmly welcome our plea's to help these beautiful children.

Our team grows each and every year and is spread out all over Michigan. Anyone that is interested in joining our team and fundraising for this wonderful cause can contact me personally by email at team.captain@teamskylerandrayna.com or visit us on the Walk for Wishes Event Page and JOIN OUR TEAM!

If you are interested in donating to the cause, we Thank You and ask that you either Contact Us or visit our Team Donation Page!
