Greetings...

Welcome to the home of Team Skyler & Rayna!

This team was built with one common purpose, to help make wishes come true for children with life threatening conditions. Make-A-Wish Foundation of Michigan grants a little more than one wish per day, for these children. It is their belief that a wish experience can be a turning point for children, helping to give them the hope and encouragement needed to continue their fight. This one belief guides them in everything they do and compels each member of this team to work hard and help!

For the past 5 years we have taken part in the Foundation’s Event: Walk for Wishes! Each year since 1999, The Make-A-Wish Foundation of Michigan has coupled with the Detroit Zoo and over 2500 generous participants to host the fundraising event: Walk for Wishes. People from all walks of life and all over Michigan, participate in this heartwarming event! Individuals, Teams, Businesses and former Wish Families set goals to raise as much money as possible for these beautiful innocent children. The common desire to help make a child’s wish come true drives us all and we embark each year with the goal to do more than the year before.

In 2013, the 15th Annual Walk for Wishes raised more than $360,000! Our Team goal was to raise $2000 and we raised a total of $3,267! In the 5 years we have rallied together, we have successfully granted one entire wish, which can cost on average over $8,000. It is my hope that this year at the 16th Annual Walk for Wishes we can raise $5000!

With your help and the help of many others just like you, we can get there! Thank you for taking the time to learn about our team and our cause, and from the bottom of our hearts at Team Skyler & Rayna, THANK YOU & GOD BLESS!
